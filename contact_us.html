Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

Does your bakery use any artificial flavourings or colourings?

Absolutely not! We use 100% fresh and real flavourings extracted from raw ingredients.

Which area do you deliver to?

Currently we only provide delivery services to customers who are in the Klang Valley area, including the Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur and Petaling Jaya. We currently do not offer international delivery service. Customers are subject to the delivery cost. However, orders in a single receipt of over RM30 will receive free delivery

Am I able to self pick-up?

Yes, self pick-up is available at our bakery at Ground Floor, Sunway University from 9 am to 7 pm. Full address here:
5, Jalan Universiti, Bandar Sunway, 47500 Petaling Jaya, Selangor.

Payment Method?

Currently, we accept online banking & Touch ’n Go eWallet.
