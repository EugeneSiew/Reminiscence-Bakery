Our Story

Started off as a concept during the Movement Control Order(MCO), Reminiscent Bakery established in early in 2022 with a team of passionate bakers led by our founder, Eugene. Reminiscent Bakery aims to rediscover traditional flavours that filled our childhood with warmth and joy at the same time sharing our passion of baking to everyone. Hence, our name was created with the combination of reminiscence + scent which resonates with our goal of bringing nostalgic and familiar tastes to everyone through our product.

What We Sell

Here at Reminiscent Bakery, we specialize in making various flavours of chiffon cake as well as swiss rolls that are 100% artificial flavorings and preservatives free. Using only the freshest of ingredients, our chiffon cakes is our go to cake because of its soft, pillowy and fluffy texture accompanied by different flavours such as chocolate and coffee that gives you the feeling of comfort while enjoying it. Our swiss rolls is constructed from our base chiffon cake recipe which is then filled with freshly whipped cream and rolled up to achieve the iconic log shape.
